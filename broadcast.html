Flora & Fauna with Marta Broadcast Package

Art Direction, Motion & Illustration

The purpose of this project was to create the main elements that make up an Original Broadcast Package. I am a huge nature lover so I chose to create a package for a nature-themed show. In this fictional series, Flora&Fauna with Marta, the viewers are lead through various forests and wooded areas in Canada and are taught about native plants and animals.
