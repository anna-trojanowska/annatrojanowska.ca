A little about Anna.

I am a graphic designer, an illustrator, and the type of person that notices the flowers bloom. I am inspired by the unconventional and I always look for a reason to laugh. My passions are illustration and concept design, and my goal is to breathe life into my work using motion graphics.

I like to embroider in my spare time. What started out as a class project quickly turned into a stress-relieving passtime. If you're interested in a commissioned piece, please visit my Etsy store or holla at yo gurl.
